Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Honest answers to the questions we hear most often.

QWhat exactly am I paying for?
You are helping SharePal purchase a specific rental asset. In return, SharePal tags that asset to your account, pays you secured monthly earnings from its business revenue, and returns the full original asset purchase cost to you as cash at tenure end.
QWhat happens at the end of the tenure?
At the end of your tenure, you receive the full original asset purchase cost back — directly to your bank account.
QWhen exactly will I receive my payouts?
On the same date you made your payment — every month for monthly plans, every year for yearly plans. This is visible on your dashboard as upcoming payout dates.
QWhy do you need my bank account details?
Solely to send your monthly payouts directly to your account. We do not use it for any other purpose.
QCan I exit the program early?
The program runs for your chosen tenure (12, 24, or 36 months). Early exit is available only on mutual consent — since benefits like 3% wallet credit are given to you immediately at enrollment.
QIs this an investment, deposit, or financial product?
It is a business arrangement. You help SharePal purchase an asset, SharePal manages all operations and shares earnings with you. It is not a deposit, mutual fund, or financial securities product.
